IPCA Explorer

The IPCA explorer is an ongoing project navigate data "sketches" generated with Geometric Multi-Resolution Analysis (GMRA) through a web-based interface. This is work in collaboration with Mauro Maggioni at Duke University

The visualization combines an icicle view, which represents the hierarchical, low-dimensional breakdown of the data at various scales, plus a new type of scatterplot representing the distribution of the data at that scale, projected into the space of the parent distribution.
